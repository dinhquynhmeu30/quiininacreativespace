Venice is a city in northeastern Italy and the capital of the Veneto region. It is situated across a group of 118 small islands that are separated by canals and linked by 400 bridges. Known for its beauty, Venice is such a popular tourist attractions. Venice is an important tourist destination foritscelebrated art and architecture. By 2017, UNESCO was considering the addition of Venice to its "In-Danger" list which includes historical ruins in war-torn countries. When you travel to Venice, tourists are amazed by its peaceful and romantic atmosphere, combine with a rich historical value.
